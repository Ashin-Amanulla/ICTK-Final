Course Description

Software testing is the art of investigating software to ensure that its quality is in line with the requirement of the client. Professionally trained software testers are in great demand nowadays and are sought after in the industry. The profile of the software tester has seen phenomenal growth. From being a test engineer, one can grow to become a senior test engineer, from a test lead to a test manager; or alternatively, one can become a QA lead or QA Manager. Moreover, the options available on the testing tools side are vast.

Objectives

  • Know the basics of SDLC

  • Familiarize with Software Testing Life Cycle

  • Understand the roles and responsibilities in testing.

  • Learn how to design,write and manage test cases.

  • Learn how to do Functional and Non-functional Testing of a software product.

  • Understand how to use Selenium Webdriver for automation testing.

  • Understand the basics of automation testing.

Requirements

  • You will need a copy of Adobe XD 2019 or above. A free trial can be downloaded from Adobe.

  • No previous design experience is needed.

  • No previous Adobe XD skills are needed.
