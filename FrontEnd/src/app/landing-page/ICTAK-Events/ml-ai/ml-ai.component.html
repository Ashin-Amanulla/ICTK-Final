What is Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) are two very hot buzzwords right now, and often seem to be used interchangeably. Artificial Intelligence is the broader concept of machines being able to carry out tasks in a way that we would consider “smart”.Machines learn and provide intelligent insights through a sophisticated use of learning algorithms which is referred to as Machine Learning. To provide business value, the machine is trained to learn patterns from data and then can proceed autonomously on new and changing data. Hence, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence jobs are high in demand and continue to be the winners in today’s world.
